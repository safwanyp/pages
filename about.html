About us

The Company

Established in the year 2010, Al Misbah United Enterprises LLC, has emerged to be as one of the largest and stockiest distributor of Bearings, Belts and Seals for Industrial and Automotive segments in the Sultanate of Oman region. By serving across multiple governorates of Oman, We have created a network which caters to the needs of our clients in a systematic and prompt manner. We are proud of our experienced and professional team who have comprehensive knowledge of the parts and can facilitate the customer needs.



Our Vision

To become a leading provider in the Industrial and Automotive segment in the Sultanate of Oman region. Cater to the needs of the clients by providing exceptional service and a comprehensive range of products
